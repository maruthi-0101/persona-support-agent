ACME CLOUD PLATFORM — ACCOUNT RECOVERY GUIDE
===============================================

If you have lost access to your Acme Cloud account, follow this guide to recover it.

1. PASSWORD RESET (Self-Service)
---------------------------------
If you know your email but forgot your password:

  a) Go to https://dashboard.acmecloud.io/forgot-password
  b) Enter the email address associated with your account.
  c) Click "Send Reset Link."
  d) Check your inbox for an email from no-reply@acmecloud.io.
  e) Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour).
  f) Set a new password meeting these requirements:
     - Minimum 12 characters
     - At least 1 uppercase letter
     - At least 1 lowercase letter
     - At least 1 number
     - At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
  g) After resetting, you will be logged in automatically.

Note: Reset emails may take up to 5 minutes. Check Spam/Junk folders.

2. EMAIL ADDRESS CHANGE
------------------------
If you no longer have access to the email on your account:

  a) Contact support@acmecloud.io from any email address.
  b) Subject line: "Email Change Request — [Your Name]"
  c) Provide the following for identity verification:
     - Full name on the account
     - Organization name (if applicable)
     - Last 4 digits of the payment method on file
     - Approximate date of account creation
     - Names of any applications deployed on the account
  d) Our team will verify your identity within 1-2 business days.
  e) Once verified, we will update your email and send confirmation.

3. TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION RECOVERY
---------------------------------------
If you lost your 2FA device and don't have backup recovery codes:

  a) Contact support@acmecloud.io with subject: "2FA Recovery — [Account Email]"
  b) You will be required to verify your identity:
     - Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license)
     - Selfie holding the ID (to prevent fraud)
     - Answer security questions set during account creation
  c) Identity verification takes 1-3 business days.
  d) Once verified, 2FA will be disabled on your account.
  e) Log in and immediately set up new 2FA credentials.

Important: We strongly recommend saving backup recovery codes in a secure location
(password manager, printed and stored in a safe, etc.) to avoid this process.

4. COMPROMISED ACCOUNT
-----------------------
If you believe your account has been accessed by an unauthorized person:

  IMMEDIATE STEPS:
  a) Reset your password immediately if you still have access.
  b) Revoke all active API keys: Dashboard → Settings → API Keys → Revoke All
  c) Review the audit log: Dashboard → Security → Audit Log
  d) Check for unauthorized changes:
     - New team members added
     - Applications created or deleted
     - Configuration changes
     - Billing information modifications

  CONTACT SUPPORT:
  a) Email security@acmecloud.io with subject: "URGENT: Compromised Account"
  b) Include:
     - Your account email
     - Description of suspicious activity
     - Approximate time you noticed the issue
  c) Our security team will respond within 2 hours.

5. DELETED ACCOUNT RECOVERY
-----------------------------
If your account was recently deleted (within 30 days):

  a) Contact support@acmecloud.io with subject: "Account Recovery — [Account Email]"
  b) Accounts can be restored within 30 days of deletion.
  c) All data (applications, configurations, databases) will be restored.
  d) After 30 days, account data is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

6. ORGANIZATION / TEAM ACCOUNT RECOVERY
-----------------------------------------
If you are locked out of an organization account:

  a) Contact your organization admin to reset your access.
  b) If the admin is unavailable, contact support@acmecloud.io.
  c) Provide your organization name and role.
  d) We may contact other verified organization admins for approval.
