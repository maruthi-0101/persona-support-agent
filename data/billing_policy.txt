ACME CLOUD PLATFORM — BILLING POLICY
=====================================

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. BILLING CYCLE
----------------
- All accounts are billed monthly on the anniversary of the account creation date.
- Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle and are due within 15 days.
- Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge after the 15-day grace period.

2. PAYMENT METHODS
------------------
Accepted payment methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- ACH / Bank Transfer (US accounts only)
- Wire Transfer (for invoices exceeding $5,000)
- PayPal (Pro plan and below)

To update your payment method:
  Dashboard → Billing → Payment Methods → Add/Edit

3. PRICING TIERS
----------------
  Plan          Monthly Price    Included Resources
  ----------    -------------    ------------------
  Free          $0               1 app, 512 MB RAM, shared CPU
  Starter       $5/month         2 apps, 1 GB RAM, 1 vCPU
  Pro           $25/month        5 apps, 4 GB RAM, 2 vCPU
  Business      $99/month        20 apps, 16 GB RAM, 4 vCPU
  Enterprise    Custom           Unlimited apps, dedicated resources

4. OVERAGE CHARGES
------------------
- Bandwidth: $0.10 per GB over the included allowance
- Storage: $0.05 per GB/month over the included allowance
- Compute: $0.04 per vCPU-hour beyond the plan limit
- API calls: $0.001 per 1,000 calls beyond the plan limit

Overage charges are calculated daily and added to the next invoice.

5. PLAN CHANGES
---------------
- Upgrades: Effective immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.
- Downgrades: Effective at the start of the next billing cycle. No partial refunds for the current cycle.
- Plan changes can be made at: Dashboard → Billing → Change Plan

6. TAXES
--------
- Prices listed are exclusive of applicable taxes.
- Sales tax, VAT, or GST may apply depending on your jurisdiction.
- Tax-exempt organizations must submit valid documentation to billing@acmecloud.io.

7. INVOICE DISPUTES
-------------------
If you believe an invoice is incorrect:
1. Contact billing@acmecloud.io within 30 days of the invoice date.
2. Provide your account ID, invoice number, and a description of the discrepancy.
3. Disputed amounts will be investigated within 5 business days.
4. Valid disputes will be credited on the next billing cycle.

8. ACCOUNT SUSPENSION
---------------------
- Accounts with payments overdue by more than 30 days may be suspended.
- Suspended accounts cannot deploy new applications or modify existing ones.
- Data is retained for 90 days after suspension. After 90 days, data may be permanently deleted.
- To reactivate a suspended account, pay all outstanding invoices plus any applicable late fees.

9. CANCELLATION
---------------
- You may cancel your account at any time from Dashboard → Settings → Cancel Account.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No refunds are issued for partial billing periods.
- All data will be deleted 30 days after cancellation unless you request a data export.

For billing questions, contact: billing@acmecloud.io
Phone: +1-800-ACME-PAY (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
