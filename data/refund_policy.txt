ACME CLOUD PLATFORM — REFUND POLICY
=====================================

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

1. ELIGIBILITY
--------------
Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

a) Service Outage Refunds:
   - If the platform experiences downtime exceeding your SLA guarantee, you are entitled to service credits.
   - Standard Plan: Refund for downtime beyond 8.7 hours/year (99.9% SLA)
   - Business Plan: Refund for downtime beyond 4.4 hours/year (99.95% SLA)
   - Enterprise Plan: Refund for downtime beyond 52 minutes/year (99.99% SLA)

b) Billing Error Refunds:
   - If you were charged incorrectly due to a system error, a full refund for the overcharge will be issued.
   - You must report billing errors within 60 days of the charge.

c) New Account Refunds:
   - New accounts are eligible for a full refund within the first 14 days of their paid subscription.
   - The 14-day refund window begins on the date of the first paid invoice.

d) Annual Plan Refunds:
   - Annual subscriptions may be refunded on a prorated basis within the first 30 days.
   - After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable but may be downgraded.

2. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
-----------------------
The following charges are NOT eligible for refunds:
- Overage charges for bandwidth, storage, or compute already consumed
- Add-on services (managed databases, Redis, Elasticsearch) after provisioning
- Domain registration fees
- Custom SSL certificate purchases
- Professional services and consulting fees
- Charges incurred during free trial period overages

3. REFUND PROCESS
-----------------
To request a refund:
1. Email refunds@acmecloud.io with subject: "Refund Request — [Account ID]"
2. Include:
   - Account ID and email address
   - Invoice number(s) for the charges in question
   - Reason for the refund request
   - Any supporting documentation (screenshots, error logs, etc.)
3. Refund requests are reviewed within 5 business days.
4. Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days.

4. REFUND METHODS
-----------------
- Credit card charges: Refunded to the original card
- ACH/Bank transfers: Refunded to the original bank account
- Wire transfers: Refunded via wire (a $25 processing fee may apply)
- Account credits: May be issued instead of a monetary refund at Acme's discretion

5. SERVICE CREDITS (SLA)
------------------------
Service credits for SLA violations:
- 99.9% to 99.0% uptime: 10% credit on monthly bill
- 99.0% to 95.0% uptime: 25% credit on monthly bill
- Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit on monthly bill

Service credits must be claimed within 30 days of the incident.
Credits are applied to the next billing cycle and do not carry over.

6. CHARGEBACKS
--------------
If you file a chargeback with your bank instead of contacting us:
- Your account will be immediately suspended pending investigation.
- A $25 chargeback processing fee will be applied.
- We reserve the right to terminate accounts with repeated chargebacks.

We strongly encourage contacting refunds@acmecloud.io before initiating a chargeback.

7. CONTACT
----------
Refund inquiries: refunds@acmecloud.io
Phone: +1-800-ACME-PAY (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST)
Response time: Within 2 business days
