ACME CLOUD PLATFORM — LOGIN TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
===================================================

If you are having trouble logging into your Acme Cloud account, follow the steps below.

1. INCORRECT PASSWORD
---------------------
Symptoms: "Invalid email or password" error message.

Steps:
  a) Verify that Caps Lock is OFF.
  b) Try typing your password in a plain text editor first to confirm accuracy.
  c) Clear your browser's cached credentials:
     - Chrome: Settings → Passwords → Find acmecloud.io → Delete saved password
     - Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Saved Logins → Remove acmecloud.io entry
  d) If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  e) Password reset emails are sent within 2 minutes. Check your Spam/Junk folder.

2. ACCOUNT LOCKED
-----------------
Symptoms: "Your account has been temporarily locked" error message.

Cause: 5 consecutive failed login attempts within 15 minutes.

Steps:
  a) Wait 30 minutes for the automatic unlock.
  b) If urgent, contact support@acmecloud.io with your account email.
  c) After unlocking, reset your password immediately.
  d) Consider enabling a password manager to prevent future lockouts.

3. TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA) ISSUES
------------------------------------------
Symptoms: Cannot receive or enter the 2FA code.

If using an Authenticator App (Google Authenticator, Authy):
  a) Ensure your device clock is synchronized (Settings → Date & Time → Auto).
  b) Time drift of more than 30 seconds can cause code rejection.
  c) Try the next code — codes rotate every 30 seconds.

If using SMS:
  a) Verify your phone number is correct in Dashboard → Settings → Security.
  b) Check if your carrier is blocking short-code messages.
  c) Request a voice call alternative by clicking "Call me instead."

Lost access to 2FA device:
  a) Use one of your backup recovery codes (provided during 2FA setup).
  b) If you don't have recovery codes, contact support@acmecloud.io.
  c) Identity verification will be required (government-issued ID + account details).
  d) Recovery process takes 1-3 business days.

4. SSO / SINGLE SIGN-ON ISSUES
-------------------------------
Symptoms: Redirect loop or "SSO configuration error" message.

Steps:
  a) Verify your organization's SSO provider is operational.
  b) Clear browser cookies for acmecloud.io and your SSO provider.
  c) Try an incognito/private browser window.
  d) Confirm your email domain matches the SSO configuration.
  e) Contact your IT admin to verify the SAML/OIDC configuration:
     - Entity ID: https://auth.acmecloud.io/saml/metadata
     - ACS URL: https://auth.acmecloud.io/saml/acs
     - Logout URL: https://auth.acmecloud.io/saml/slo

5. BROWSER COMPATIBILITY
-------------------------
Supported browsers:
  - Google Chrome 90+
  - Mozilla Firefox 90+
  - Microsoft Edge 90+
  - Safari 15+

If you experience rendering issues:
  a) Update your browser to the latest version.
  b) Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers and privacy extensions).
  c) Clear your browser cache: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Cmd+Shift+Delete on macOS).
  d) Try a different browser to isolate the issue.

6. SESSION TIMEOUT
------------------
Default session duration: 8 hours of inactivity.

If your session expires frequently:
  a) Check if your browser is configured to clear cookies on exit.
  b) Ensure "Remember me" is checked during login.
  c) Enterprise accounts can configure custom session durations:
     Dashboard → Organization → Security → Session Timeout

7. STILL NEED HELP?
--------------------
If none of the above steps resolved your issue:
  - Email: support@acmecloud.io
  - Live Chat: Available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
  - Emergency: +1-800-ACME-SOS (Enterprise accounts, 24/7)

Please include:
  - Your account email address
  - Browser name and version
  - Screenshot of the error message
  - Approximate time of the issue
